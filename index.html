E.P All Day!! Minnesota Pho Bowl!!!

Inside The Playbook

Coaching Staffs

This Pho Bowl Rice Noodle Soup system is run on beef broth flavor surrounded by great ingredients:

  • Cinnamon Sticks
  • Beef Marrow Bones
  • Onions
  • Gingers
  • Star Anise Pods
  • Fish Sauce
  • Fennel and Coriander Seeds

There is so many schemes to go about it in this all around season. The most effective one is especially after a long night of alcohol consumption. So good and refreshing as just a WIN!

I will never get tire of it!

Depth Chart

  1. Beef Sirloin(lean)
  2. Cilantro
  3. Culantro
  4. Basil
  5. Bean Sprouts
  6. Hoisin Sauce
  7. Jalepeno
  8. Lime Wedge
  9. Red Chilli Oil
  10. Sriracha Sauce
