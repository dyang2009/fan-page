Pho

What is Pho?

Pho is a Vietnamese noodle soup base on beef marrow bone broth flavoring and spices with thinly slice beef and meatballs.

How do I eat my pho?

I like to eat spicy so I will usually add chilli garlic oil paste and sriracha sauce into my pho bowl. Other condiments I use is cilantro, culantro, basil, hoisin sauce, and lime wedges.Hoisin sauce will add just a sweetness into my pho so that my pho bowl will still have a nice balance.

Why Pho?

The smell of the broth is just so fresh which feels warm and relaxing. Pho sets the mood in a positively way such as a sunny day. A bowl of pho will actually fill my stomache up. Eating pho at restaurants is affordable, but even better when it is home-made because I can add more spices into the pho broth and eat as much as I want.

Watermelon

Why I like watermelon?

The taste is so refreshing. It is sweet and juicy when taking the first bite into the watermelon. Having it on a sunny day at an outdoor event is common to have watermelon serve there, but for me I have it anywhere as long as the seasons approves it.
